The Starbrand Marshals

Affiliated Universe

Multiversal Nexus (Headquartered near the edge of known space)

Core Purpose & Philosophy

The Starbrand Marshals are a rugged intergalactic enforcement faction tasked with arresting high-threat cosmic fugitives and sealing away dangerous entities in the Vault of Justice — a universal prison. Their creed blends frontier law with cosmic accountability, and they are known for upholding justice across war-torn star systems.

Known Specialties

  • High-risk criminal apprehension
  • Energy suppression technologies
  • Dimensional bounty tracking
  • Ancient interstellar lawkeeping

Key Members

  • Roan “Ironsight” Kelvar – Veteran sharpshooter and tactical leader
  • Mysa Quell – Psychic Sentinel and dual-energy whip expert
  • Redd Vox – Kinetican enforcer with an integrated arm cannon
  • Sorn – Reformed bounty hunter with sentient tracking badge
  • Warden Halek – Keeper of the Vault, race unknown, aura suppression master

Known Rivalries

  • Criminal syndicates and dark multiversal warlords
  • The Ashborn Circle (frequent clashes)
  • Fugitives from the Vault of Justice

Notable History

Forged from the ashes of collapsed star-law systems, the Starbrand Marshals were united by a cosmic pact to prevent chaos from spilling across galaxies. Their star-shaped insignia is feared among fugitives and celebrated by liberated worlds. The Marshals operate with absolute authority in the pursuit of justice.

Alignment

Heroic Faction – Cosmic enforcers of law and containment

Paradox Realm

Overview

The Paradox Realm is a fragmented dimension of warped timelines, infinite echoes, and recursive anomalies. Logic bends and reality loops as moments fracture and coexist. It is a realm of controlled entropy and unpredictable causality, ruled by the vigilance of temporal discipline and multiversal foresight.

Overseer

Vigilus – Guardian Entity of Time and the Multiverse

Dominant Energies

Temporal Flux, Chrono Energy, Dimensional Drift, Loop Resonance, and Fracture Fields.

Inhabitant Races

Watchers of the Veil, Riftwalkers, Chrono-Shades, Timebound Avatars, and Temporal Archivists.

Key Factions

The Keepers of Time, Fractaline Order, Riftborne Observers, and the Temporal Conclave.

Significance

The Paradox Realm is the linchpin of multiversal continuity and divergence. Vigilus oversees the interwoven timelines and watches for paradoxes that could fracture entire realities. It is both the cradle of prophecy and the grave of forgotten futures.

Realm of Ruin

Overview

The Realm of Ruin is a decaying, unstable realm where broken energies, forgotten creations, and corrupted spirits wander endlessly. The land pulses with red mist, shattered geometry, and inverted gravity — a visual representation of despair itself.

Overseer

Ruin — The Lord of Despair

Dominant Energies

Despair Energy, Corruptive Echo Fields, Rot-Based Energetics, Forbidden Dark Energy

Inhabitant Races

Blightkin, Rotborn Shamans, Nullspawn, Ruin Wraiths, Fragmented Souls

Key Factions

Ashborn Circle, Cult of the Blighted, Despair Forgers, Hollow Sigil Keepers

Notable Traits

  • Constant entropy: energy, matter, and time collapse
  • Home to the Despair Cults and Ashborn remnants
  • Hosts forbidden artifacts like the Crimson Vow and Nullbirth Seed
  • Certain zones exist in recursive loops of emotional collapse

Strategic Role

The Realm of Ruin stands as a paradoxical hub — chaotic, yet containing knowledge about energy degradation, soul corrosion, and forbidden Nexus studies. It is feared, avoided, but occasionally sought out for catastrophic rituals or lost secrets.

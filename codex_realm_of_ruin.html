Realm of Ruin

Domain of Despair, Fracture, and Eternal Corruption
Classification:
Core Universe within the Sir Senju Multiverse
Overseen By:
Ruin — The Lord of Despair
Nature and Atmosphere:
A decaying, unstable realm where broken energies, forgotten creations, and corrupted spirits wander endlessly. The land pulses with red mist, shattered geometry, and inverted gravity — a visual representation of despair itself.
Notable Traits:
• Constant entropy: energy, matter, and time collapse
• Home to the Despair Cults and Ashborn remnants
• Hosts forbidden artifacts like the Crimson Vow and Nullbirth Seed
• Certain zones exist in recursive loops of emotional collapse
Strategic Role:
Realm of Ruin stands as a paradoxical hub — chaotic, yet containing knowledge about energy degradation, soul corrosion, and forbidden Nexus studies. It is feared, avoided, but occasionally sought out for catastrophic rituals or lost secrets.

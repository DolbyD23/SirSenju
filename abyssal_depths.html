Abyssal Depths

Overview

The Abyssal Depths is a universe steeped in annihilation, decay, and rebirth through destruction. It embodies entropy and the inevitable unraveling of form. It is the dominion of those who seek to consume or transcend limitation through absolute void. Time and space distort and coil inward here, feeding on themselves.

Overseer

Oblivion – Primordial of Destruction

Dominant Energies

Forbidden Dark Energy, Despair Energy, Void, Chaos-infused Energetics, and Entropic currents.

Inhabitant Races

Darkborn, Hollowed, Rift-Evolved Predators, Echo Wraiths, and Eldritch-corrupted beings.

Key Factions

The Order of the Abyss, Nullborn Acolytes, Shadow Syndicate, and the Cult of the Blighted.

Significance

While feared, the Abyssal Depths is necessary to the cycle of reality. Oblivion's dominion ensures balance through endings, wiping corrupted worlds and resetting failed timelines. It is also where many forbidden rituals originate, tempting power-hungry seekers from across the multiverse.

Celestial Sphere

Overview

The Celestial Sphere is a universe of divine order, heavenly light, and stellar majesty. It is a realm where dawn never ends, stars resonate with purpose, and celestial patterns dictate harmony. Beings here are attuned to fate, prophecy, and illumination through wisdom and radiance.

Overseer

Aurora – Celestial Deity of Dawn

Dominant Energies

Light Energy, Celestial Energy, Prophetic Flow, Radiant Chi, and Stellar Essence.

Inhabitant Races

Celestians, Luminaris, Starborn Kin, Divinecasters, and Solar-Walkers.

Key Factions

The Celestial Guardians, Circle of Prophecy, Council of Daybreak, and the Dawnforged.

Significance

The Celestial Sphere serves as the beacon of destiny and cosmic rhythm. Prophets, lightweavers, and spirit emissaries gather here to interpret celestial alignments and divine will. Aurora's eternal dawn grants clarity and purpose to many cross-universal civilizations.

The Energetic Guardians

Affiliated Universe

Elysium Nexus (active across the Multiversal Nexus)

Core Purpose & Philosophy

The Energetic Guardians are an elite assembly of warriors, scholars, and protectors entrusted with defending the balance of Energetics across all realms. Bound by honor and mastery of energy forms, they serve as both mentors and enforcers, shielding the Nexus from corruption while cultivating the next generation of energy wielders.

Known Specialties

  • Mastery of all recognized energy types
  • Cross-universal energy training and discipline
  • Nexus-based anomaly detection
  • Forbidden energy containment

Notable History

Formed in the aftermath of the first Nexus flare, the Energetic Guardians emerged as a unifying force of discipline and resistance. Their founding principles were laid by ancient masters in response to forbidden surge outbreaks. Over generations, they’ve become the primary bulwark against multiversal energy instability.

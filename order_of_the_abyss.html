The Order of the Abyss

Affiliated Universe

The Abyss (Operates through dread temples and void citadels)

Core Purpose & Philosophy

The Order of the Abyss is a malevolent faction sworn to the service of the Dark Lords, especially Umbros, Lord of Shadows. Their creed is rooted in despair, entropy, and the unmaking of universal balance. The Order exists to unravel the Nexus, devour light, and usher eternal night.

Known Specialties

  • Shadow rituals and body sacrifices
  • Despair-channeling combat techniques
  • Forbidden summoning and dark relics
  • Spread of void corruption and energy decay

Subgroups

  • The Ashborn Circle – Elite assassin squad
  • Nullborn Acolytes – Devotees to despair rituals
  • Ash Rotters – Reanimated corpses of failed initiates

Known Leaders

  • Umbros – Dark Lord of Shadows
  • Deaengelo Senju – Fallen Senju, internal corrupter
  • High Acolyte Marzareth – Ritual Overseer (unseen)

Known Rivalries

  • The Energetic Guardians
  • The Vigilant Watchers
  • All Nexus Wielders

Notable History

The Order's presence predates the Multiversal Surge. From abyssal depths they rose, seeding chaos wherever the Nexus shines brightest. Rituals in forgotten temples empowered their leaders with eldritch corruption. The Order thrives in despair, their influence expanding with each crack in reality.

Alignment

Villainous Faction – Sworn to Umbros, Enemy of Balance

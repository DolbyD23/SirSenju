The Ashborn Circle

Affiliated Universe

The Abyss (Operates multiversally through shadow gates and void rituals)

Core Purpose & Philosophy

The Ashborn Circle is an elite hit squad serving the Order of the Abyss and the Dark Lord Umbros. They specialize in high-priority assassinations, Nexus disruption, and forbidden artifact retrieval. Their belief system is rooted in chaos, entropy, and the extinguishing of Nexus harmonics. Each member is bound to the Void Pact, granting immense power in exchange for fragments of their soul.

  • Stealth and infiltration across realms
  • Nexus sabotage and artifact theft
  • Void and shadow manipulation

Notable History

Born from the core of the Order of the Abyss, the Ashborn Circle was formed to execute missions deemed too volatile or forbidden for traditional forces. Their rise coincided with a wave of multiversal unrest, and their presence signals incoming devastation. Sightings of the Ashborn Circle often precede Nexus corruption or the disappearance of ancient artifacts.

Alignment

Antagonistic Faction – Aligned with Umbros and the entropy of the Abyss

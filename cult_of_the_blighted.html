The Cult of the Blighted

Affiliated Universe

Realm of Ruin

Core Purpose & Philosophy

The Cult of the Blighted thrives on suffering, decay, and despair. Formed from the remnants of shattered ideologies, they believe in the cleansing power of rot — that through decay, the multiverse is reborn. They spread corruption as a sacrament, their members often willingly undergoing physical blight to grow more powerful.

Known Specialties

  • Plaguecraft and rot rituals
  • Despair-induced illusions
  • Blight-forging armor and weapons
  • Conversion through corruption

Key Members

  • Faelix the Plague Tongue – Blightspeaker and corrupter
  • Ny’shara the Hollowed Bride – Haunting deceiver and soul flayer
  • Zaryk the Tailbreaker – Bone-warped executioner

Known Rivalries

  • The Harmony Alliance
  • The Vigilant Watchers

Notable History

Born in the blackened wastes of the Realm of Ruin, the Cult of the Blighted grew from failed experiments and broken warlocks. Their infection spreads from planet to soul, a creeping shadow of sickness that even the Nexus struggles to purify.

Alignment

Villainous Faction – Decay-worshiping plague cult of the Realm of Ruin

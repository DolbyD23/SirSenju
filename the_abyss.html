The Abyss

Overview

The Abyss is a realm of despair incarnate — a suffocating domain where suffering, madness, and nihilism flourish. Time fractures, landscapes bleed, and souls erode in the grip of Ruin’s dominion. It is a universe not meant to thrive, but to wither, drawing those who seek ultimate release or forbidden purpose.

Overseer

Ruin – Dark Lord of Despair

Dominant Energies

Despair Energy, Forbidden Dark Energy, Corruption Streams, and Echoes of Loss.

Inhabitant Races

Hollowed, Wretched, Echoborn, Mal'kin, Despair-forged Beasts, and Nullbound.

Key Factions

Despair Cults, Order of the Abyss, Crimson Dirge, and the Last Mourning Choir.

Significance

The Abyss tests the spirit of every traveler. Those who return are never the same. Ruin’s reach infects not only matter but memory and meaning. It is said that even time forgets itself here — a place where the multiverse stores its grief.

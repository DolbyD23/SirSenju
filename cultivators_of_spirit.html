Cultivators of the Spirit

Affiliated Universe

Elysian Gardens (Centered in sacred meditation temples and spiritual monoliths)

Core Purpose & Philosophy

The Cultivators of the Spirit believe in the eternal growth of one's inner energy through reflection, balance, and connection with the world around them. Their teachings prioritize clarity of mind, purification of aura, and harmonious combat. They often guide young warriors toward paths of enlightenment.

Known Specialties

  • Spiritual meditation and healing arts
  • Chi manipulation and aura refinement
  • Barrier creation and spiritual warding
  • Combat rooted in grace, flow, and inner calm

Key Members

  • Master Shuyan – Grand Spirit Mentor
  • Lyra Moonshadow – Spirit empath and astral conduit
  • Tomo Kenzai – Youth counselor and essence reader

Known Rivalries

  • The Order of the Abyss
  • Void-wielding factions
  • Any force that disturbs inner harmony

Notable History

The Cultivators have existed since the Age of Bloom, maintaining sanctuaries across Elysian Gardens. Their influence on young martial artists is profound, often serving as the spiritual backbone behind many Nexus-wielding heroes. Though peaceful, their inner strength is boundless when balance is broken.

Alignment

Heroic Faction – Guardians of Inner Peace and Aura Harmony

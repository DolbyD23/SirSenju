Galeon Tides

Overview

The Galeon Tides is a vast aquatic universe of depthless oceans, sky rivers, and living currents. It is ruled by the rhythm of tides and the spirit of flowing life. Storms, tsunamis, and tranquil stillness coexist in fluid harmony. Home to water-borne civilizations and guardians of marine Energetics, it is a universe of motion and adaptation.

Overseer

Aquara – Elemental Lady of Water

Dominant Energies

Water Energy, Tidal Chi, Ethereal Mist, Stormflow Energetics, and Aqua Harmony Currents.

Inhabitant Races

Murkfolk, Tidewalkers, Aquari, Sky-Floaters, Tidelore Sentients, and Hydrokinetic creatures.

Key Factions

The Tidal Guardians, Abyssal Navigators, Oceanheart Collective, and the Pearl Accord.

Significance

The Galeon Tides serves as the multiversal memory of water and fluid evolution. It sustains the life essence of countless realities. Aquara’s domain trains many elemental users and mystics who seek serenity through change. Here, all things flow — and all returns to the sea.

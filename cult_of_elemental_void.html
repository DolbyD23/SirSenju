Cult of the Elemental Void

Affiliated Universe

Elementaria (Fractured Realms)

Core Purpose & Philosophy

The Cult of the Elemental Void seeks to unravel the natural harmony of elemental energy. Believing that the original elements were tainted by the Arbiter’s order, they worship the primal chaos that existed before form. They summon volatile beings, distort pure elements, and aim to reforge the universe in elemental entropy.

Known Specialties

  • Fusion of elemental and void energy
  • Summoning of corrupted elementals
  • Rituals of entropy and energy inversion
  • Terraforming battlegrounds into unstable rift zones

Key Members

  • High Wretch Ka’Thar – Prophet of Elemental Reversal
  • Syra the Fractured Gale – Wind-Flame hybridist
  • Mol’thex the Stone Maw – Earth-Shadow tank
  • Zerani – Ritual conduit of Elemental Collapse

Known Rivalries

  • Elemental Concord
  • Harmony Alliance

Notable History

The Cult’s rise coincided with elemental rifts tearing open across Elementaria. Their corrupted rituals created hybrids too unstable for most planes to host, forcing them into nomadic cults traveling between fractured zones. Despite being disorganized, they are devastatingly effective in destabilizing planetary cores.

Alignment

Chaotic Antagonists – Radical entropy worshippers of corrupted elemental origin

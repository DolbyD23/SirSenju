Umbral Cults

Affiliated Universe

The Abyss

Core Purpose & Philosophy

The Umbral Cults are shadow-bound sects that serve the Dark Lord Umbros, drawing their strength from despair, corruption, and forbidden shadow rituals. Operating in secrecy, they believe in unraveling the light of creation and replacing it with eternal gloom. Their members embrace fear as a weapon and despair as sacred doctrine.

Known Specialties

  • Shadow sigils and despair-charged incantations
  • Energy siphoning through emotional collapse
  • Corruption of light and hope-based energies
  • Ritual summoning of dark entities

Key Members

  • Priest of Withering Light – Speaker of the Abyss
  • The Wane Heralds – Cultists who erase positive energy auras
  • Shadowbinders – Specialists in dream corruption and tethering

Known Rivalries

  • Celestial Guardians
  • The Harmony Alliance
  • Energetic Guardians

Notable History

Tracing back to the first eclipse of Elysian Gardens, the Umbral Cults rose when Umbros whispered truths through the shadow veil. Their rituals have collapsed cities into madness and forged gateways between nightmares and waking reality. Their cult branches exist in almost every universe, hiding in forgotten ruins and echo chambers of sorrow.

Alignment

Villainous Faction – Fanatical servants of Umbros, thriving on shadow and despair
